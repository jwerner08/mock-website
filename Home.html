About

Jakob Werner is a guitarist, musician, and singer-songwriter who has over a decade’s worth of experience playing and performing music. Beginning his musical journey, Jakob Studied under local Lansing, Michigan musicians Elden Kelly and Derek Houle, where he quickly became passionate and deeply involved in music. To further pursue his musical dreams, Jakob moved from Michigan to Colorado with the sole purpose of graduating with a degree in music from the University of Colorado Denver. While attending the University of Colorado Denver, Jakob studied under highly acclaimed Colorado musicians such as Dan Schwindt (The Motet, Kyle Hollingsworth Band), and Greg Garrison (Leftover Salmon). Jakob was also a part of the University of Colorado Denver’s Bluegrass and Django Jazz ensembles. Having graduated Summa Cum Laude of his class, Jakob is committed to helping others discover the same joy and passion that led him into his musical journey.
